How to prepare for the delivery of biomaterial for cytological studies?

Smears (scrapings) from the surface of the cervix (external uterine os) and cervical canal for atypia

❗ The analysis is recommended to be taken no earlier than on the 5th day of the menstrual cycle and no later than 5 days before the expected start of menstruation

❗ Avoid sexual intercourse 24 hours before the test

❗ Exclude the use of lubricants, tampons or spermicides, douching, the introduction of medicines, suppositories, creams into the vagina 2 days before the analysis

Aspirate from the uterine cavity

☑️ The analysis is recommended to be taken no earlier than on the 6th–9th day of the menstrual cycle and no later than on the 5th day before the expected onset of menstruation

☑️ Douching should not be performed, and intravaginal therapy should be avoided for 24 hours prior to analysis

☑️ All manipulations in the uterine cavity can be carried out only if the infectious diseases of the mucous membrane of the vagina and cervix are completely cured. It is necessary to make sure that there is no pregnancy, vaginitis or cervicitis
