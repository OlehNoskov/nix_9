How to prepare for blood tests?

Before donating blood, it is recommended:

⚠️ Do not eat fatty foods on the eve of the analysis.

⚠️ Do not eat 8-12 hours before the test.

⚠️Drink 1-2 glasses of plain non-carbonated water in the morning for 40-60 minutes. before analysis. Viscosity will decrease, and it will be easier to take blood for analysis.

Before donating blood:

☑️ In agreement with the doctor, stop taking medications at least a day before the analysis

☑️ Avoid taking dietary supplements containing biotin (vitamin H, vitamin B7)

Before donating blood, it is recommended:

☑️ Avoid physical activity for at least 30 minutes prior to testing

☑️ Eliminate increased emotional stress, anxiety, stress

☑️ A few minutes before the blood sampling procedure, take a comfortable position (sit down), relax, calm down

Before donating blood, you must:

✔️ Avoid drinking alcohol for 72 hours prior to testing

✔️ Do not smoke for at least 30 minutes before the test
