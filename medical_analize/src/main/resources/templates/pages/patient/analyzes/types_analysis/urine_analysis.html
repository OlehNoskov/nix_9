How to prepare for urine tests?

📌️ It is not recommended to consume on the eve of the study (10-12 hours before): alcohol, spicy, salty foods, foods that change the color of urine (for example, beets, carrots)

📌️ Avoid taking diuretics

📌️ Before taking the analysis, make a thorough toilet of the external genitalia

📌️ For women, the study is recommended to be performed before menstruation or 2 days after it ends.
