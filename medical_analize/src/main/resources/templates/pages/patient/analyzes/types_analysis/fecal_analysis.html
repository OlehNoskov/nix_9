How to prepare for a stool test?

Before donating blood, it is recommended:

☑️ Do not eat fatty foods on the eve of the analysis

☑️ Do not eat 8-12 hours before the test

☑️ Drink 1-2 glasses of plain non-carbonated water in the morning for 40-60 minutes. before analysis. Viscosity will decrease, and it will be easier to take blood for analysis

Why is it important?

☑️ Eating on the eve of the analysis can distort the result and even lead to the impossibility of performing the test.

☑️ After the absorption of nutrients from food, the concentration of proteins, fats, carbohydrates and other compounds in the blood increases, the work of enzymes and some hormones starts, and blood viscosity may change.

☑️ These factors may affect the concentration of the test substance or alter the properties of the blood itself in a way that interferes with the analysis.
