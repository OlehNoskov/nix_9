D-Dimer

D-dimer is a fragment of a protein that appears during the breakdown of a blood clot, which occurs during blood clotting and is considered a marker of blood clots.

Blood clots in blood vessels are found in a number of diseases: varicose, atrial fibrillation, with a complicated course of infectious diseases, complications after surgery and can lead to death.
